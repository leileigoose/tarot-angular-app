About this mini project

What does this app do?

This app will let you pull a random tarot card and will give you a quick summary on the card you pulled based on a question you asked.

Who am I?

Why did I create this app?

This has always been a web app that I wanted to make, but the big push to get started was getting an interview with HDS.
Their job listing had asked for Angular experience, so I wanted to familiarize myself with the framework.
This was something I did in 2 days, and I'm happy with how it's starting to look!
I hope to continue to improve it--adding more features, asking my artist friends to help with design cards, and just making it something fun to use!
